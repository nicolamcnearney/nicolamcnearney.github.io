Since its launch in 1997, Japanese Outboard & Spares Ltd. has been the go-to local marine technician.

David McNearney first started Japanese Outboard & Spares Ltd. after returning to New Zealand after a decade in Japan. Finding a place in the industry that was yet to be filled, he started his business in remanufacturing Yamaha and Tohatsu outboard engines. After over 20 years in the business, he has now progressed to rebuilding and trading with genuine and aftermarket parts.
